Kimbap Recipe

Description

Kimbap (김밥) is a Korean dish made of rice and assorted fillings rolled in dried seaweed (gim), then sliced into bite-sized pieces. It’s often compared to sushi rolls, but the flavor profile is distinctively Korean, featuring sesame oil-seasoned rice and ingredients like pickled radish, spinach, carrots, egg, and sometimes beef or tuna.
