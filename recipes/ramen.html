Steps

  • In a pot, combine chicken stock, water, soy sauce, mirin, sugar, garlic, and ginger.
  • Simmer gently for 30–45 minutes.
  • Strain the broth (optional) to remove solids.
  • Boil eggs for 6–7 minutes.
  • Cool in ice water, peel, and marinate in soy sauce + mirin mix for 1+ hour.
  • Cook the Ramen Noodles
  • Prepare Toppings
  • Assemble the Bowl
  • Serve hot
