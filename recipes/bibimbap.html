Bibimbap's recipe

Description

Bibimbap (비빔밥) is a large bowl of rice topped with an array of individually prepared vegetables and meat and served with a gochujang (고추장) sauce. Bibim means mixing, and bap means rice. The mixing usually happens at the table by the diner.

ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked short-grain rice (preferably warm)
  • Vegetables
  • 100g ground beef or sliced beef (or tofu/egg for vegetarian)
  • Sauce
  • Seasoning

Steps

  • Mix gochujang, sesame oil, sugar, vinegar, and garlic in a small bowl. Adjust spice to taste.
  • Beef: Sauté ground or sliced beef with soy sauce, sugar, garlic, and sesame oil until cooked.
  • Fry the Egg
  • Assemble the Bowl
  • Place fried egg in the center.
  • Drizzle gochujang sauce on top.
  • Mix and Eat!
