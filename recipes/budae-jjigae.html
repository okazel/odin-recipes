Budae Jjigae

Description

Budae Jjigae (부대찌개) is a Korean stew, often called “Army Base Stew,” made with a mix of Korean and American ingredients like kimchi, gochujang (spicy chili paste), Spam, sausages, baked beans, instant noodles, and cheese.
